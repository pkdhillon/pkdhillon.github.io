How to make Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is my favorite cookie recipe. Check out the full details here.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix butter, sugars, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake 8–10 minutes. Cool and enjoy!
